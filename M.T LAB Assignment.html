Career Opportunities

With a Computer Engineering degree from KNUST, these are some of the exciting career paths available:

Designing, coding, testing, and maintaining software applications for various platforms and industries. This could range from mobile apps to enterprise systems.

Designing and developing computer hardware components, including circuit boards, processors, memory devices, and integrated systems.

Creating specialized computing systems embedded in electronic devices, from consumer electronics to industrial equipment.

Developing systems and applications that utilize AI and machine learning algorithms to solve complex problems and automate processes.
